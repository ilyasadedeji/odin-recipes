Cranberry Sauce

This cranberry sauce recipe uses fresh cranberries, sugar, and orange juice to make a Thanksgiving classic.

GCranberry-Sauce Ingredients

  • Fresh Cranberries
  • Sugar
  • Orange-juice

How to Make Cranberry Sauce

  1. Heat orange juice in a saucepan. Stir in sugar and cook until it dissolves.
  2. Add cranberries and continue cooking, stirring often, until they start to pop.
  3. Remove the sauce from heat and transfer it to a bowl the cranberry sauce will continue to thicken as it cools.
